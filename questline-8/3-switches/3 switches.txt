1. Turn on the first switch and leave it on for 5-10 minutes so that the bulb would heat up. 
2. After that, turn off the first switch and immediately turn on the second switch.

Now go into the room, there will be three chances:

- If the bulb is lit, Switch 2 controls the bulb.
- If the bulb is off but warm, Switch 1 controls the bulb.
- If the bulb is off and cool, Switch 3 controls the bulb.
